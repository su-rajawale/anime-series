Television/Webseries

One-Punch Man is a Japanese anime series set in City Z, the story focuses on Saitama, a superhero who has grown bored as he has become so powerful that all of his battles end in a single punch.

Fullmetal Alchemist: When a failed alchemical ritual leaves brothers Edward and Alphonse Elric with severely damaged bodies, they begin searching for the one thing that can save them; the fabled philosopher's stone.

Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood: Two brothers search for a Philosopher's Stone after an attempt to revive their deceased mother goes awry and leaves them in damaged physical forms.

Death Note: Light Yagami, a teen genius who discovers a mysterious notebook: the "Death Note", which belonged to the Shinigami Ryuk, and grants the user the supernatural ability to kill anyone whose name is written in its pages.

My Hero Academia: Izuku Midoriya, a boy born without superpowers (called Quirks) in a world where they have become commonplace, but who still dreams of becoming a superhero himself.

Mob Psycho 100 (Japanese: モブサイコ100, Hepburn: Mobu Saiko Hyaku) is a Japanese anime series based on the webcomic created by One. The story follows a psychic middle school boy who tries to live a normal life and keep his growing powers under control, even though he constantly gets into trouble.

Neon Genesis Evangelion (Japanese: 新世紀エヴァンゲリオン, Hepburn: Shinseiki Evangerion, lit. "New Century Gospel") is a Japanese mecha anime television series centerd around a teenage boy who finds himself recruited as a member of an elite team of pilots by his father.

Psycho-Pass (Japanese: サイコパス) is a Japanese cyberpunk psychological thriller anime television series about policewoman Akane Tsunemori who obeys the ruling, computerized, precognitive Sibyl System. But when she faces a criminal mastermind who can elude this "perfect" system, she questions both Sibyl and herself.

Tokyo Ghoul (Japanese: 東京喰種 トーキョーグール , Hepburn: Tōkyō Gūru) is a Japanese 12-episode anime television series about a Tokyo college student who is attacked by a ghoul, a superpowered human who feeds on human flesh. He survives, but has become part ghoul and becomes a fugitive on the run.

Another is a 2012 Japanese horror anime television series, the series is set in 1998 and focuses on a boy named Kōichi Sakakibara who, upon transferring into Yomiyama Middle School and meeting the curious Mei Misaki, finds himself in a mystery revolving around students and people related to his class facing gruesome and senseless deaths.
